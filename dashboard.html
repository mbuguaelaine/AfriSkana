How AfriSkana Works

Our automated, three-stage process for comprehensive network vulnerability assessment.

1. Scanning Phase

Our automated Python script built around Nmap probes your target IP address, identifying all open ports and running services to pinpoint specific risks.

2. Intelligence Phase

Our proprietary Risk Mapper, powered by data from the National Vulnerability Database (NVD), acts as the system's brain. It instantly assigns an alert level and provides clear, prioritized recommendations for every detected service.

3. Safety Switch

We protect hospital operations by offering both Standard Mode (full audit) and Privacy Mode (essential ports only) to ensure non-disruptive scanning.

About Us

The mission and vision behind AfriSkana.

Our Mission

AfriSkana is a web-based application designed to simplify network security for healthcare institutions across Africa. We operate as an automated, virtual security consultant that focuses on providing accessible, actionable, and privacy-first vulnerability scanning services.

Our Commitment

We believe every healthcare provider, regardless of size or location, deserves strong digital defenses. Our platform removes the complexity and cost barriers associated with traditional security audits, offering an easy-to-use solution for critical infrastructure protection.

Privacy Policy

Your data, your control

Zero Data Retention

AfriSkana is built with privacy as our core principle. We do not store, log, or retain any scan data, results, or network information.

What We Don't Collect

  • Scan results or vulnerability reports
  • Target IP addresses
  • Network topology information
  • Historical scan data
  • Port configurations

Session-Only Data

All scan results exist only in your browser's memory during your current session. When you:

  • Close the browser tab
  • Refresh the page
  • Click "Clear Results"

All data is immediately and permanently deleted.

Your Control

You have complete control over your security data. Download reports for your records, and rest assured that we never keep copies on our servers.
